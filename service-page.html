How We Work?

  • Requirement Analysis
  • Design & Prototype
  • Development
  • Testing & Quality Assurance
  • Maintenance and Support

Requirement Analysis

This phase consists of all the work that needs to be done from our end before the development begins. We identify key objectives & make informed decisions based on your project requirements. Priorities are defined, and necessary parameters are set.

Design & Prototype

Designs and mockups are conceptualized to help define the inputs and tools required for product development. We develop high-fidelity wireframes and prototypes that align with the overall vision and finalize a sound system design.

Development

In the development phase, the developers work on building different features according to the release cycles & keep improving the product. Depending on the plan, you may or may not choose to test the market with a MVP containing the core features

Testing & Quality Assurance

Several rounds of testing involving code improvements, bug fixes, feature testing, etc., are carried out for performance improvement and quality assurance. This phase aims to ensure functionality, ease of use, and appeal of the overall product.

Maintenance and Support

After launching the product in the mass market, we provide post-launch monitoring and support. Based on product usage and client feedback, we work on adding new features and improving the existing ones to better the product and the user experience.
