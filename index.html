Hiring process

We have a very focused hire-train deploy model. We only hire software engineers, we only train on web and mobile technologies (no BD, no sales, no BPO, no distractions) and we only deploy our engineers with new age tech companies and startups.

Bootcamps

Our bootcamps are very intense affairs- 6 days a week, 10-12 hours a day of coding and code reviews. By the time engineers finish the bootcamp, they have written 4-6K loc.They study whatever is required to produce that output from the best content available on the web

Process

Our deployment is a very easy process. For each new customer, we start with only 1-2 engineers chosen by matching the engineer strengths to your tech team requirements. Once you like them, we do a 12-14 months staffing contract. Essentially, we have taken all the risk away from the customer and taken it on ourselves- because we know that our fantastic engineers are anyway zero risk

Enagement

Simple commercial terms- INR50K per month* for the deployment period, INR50K absorption fees if you hire the engineer after the deployment period. This is exclusive of GST. This is pricing for Ahmedabad. Pricing for other cities is slightly higher
